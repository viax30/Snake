How To Play Snake Game

Instruction:

  • Use the arrows keys to direct the snake around the screen and eat the dots/apple.
  • The more the dots/apple the snake eats, the longer it grows and the higher your score.
  • Avoid causing the snake to eat itself, and use any keys to start the game again.
NOTE: If your score is zero it will not show in the score list.
